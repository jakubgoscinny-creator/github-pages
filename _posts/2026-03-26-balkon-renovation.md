My balcony is small — just **4.6 m²** — but I've been deep in planning mode trying to turn it into something worth stepping out onto. Here's a breakdown of where I'm at.

## The Foundation: Acacia Deck Tiles

Every concept starts with the same base: **acacia wood deck tiles (30×30 cm)**, roughly 55 pieces to cover the surface. Best price I found is direct from [4iQ](https://4iq.com.pl) at ~8.49 zł/szt — total around **467 zł**. Castorama sells them too but for nearly double.

The tiles click together with no tools, which makes this the easiest win of the whole project.

## The Greenery (shared across all concepts)

- **Lavender** (2× 6-pack from Castorama, ~40 zł) — low maintenance, smells amazing
- **Common ivy** × 3–5 plants + a **180×90 cm trellis** (~140 zł) — climbing along the railing over time
- **2× Berberis 60 cm anthracite planters** with water reservoirs (~80 zł)

## Four Concepts, One Balcony

### Concept A — IVAR 30cm (~1 546 zł)
The most open layout. An **IKEA IVAR 89×30×179 cm shelf** (330 zł) leaves 77 cm of walking space. Paired with a half-round folding table (GoodHome Lyco, 148 zł) and two folding metal chairs. Functional, affordable, airy.

### Concept B — IVAR 50cm (~1 678 zł)
Swap to the deeper **IVAR 89×50×179** (480 zł) and replace the chairs with a **Keter Comfy 270L storage bench** (248 zł) — seating AND hidden storage for cushions, tools, whatever. Walking space drops to 57 cm but the functionality jumps.

### Concept C — Scandinavian Hygge Premium (~2 038 zł)
Concept A base + mood lighting: a **solar filament string light** (70 zł), **bamboo solar lantern** (70 zł), and **solar balustrade lights** × 4 (30 zł). Add a **GoodHome Petalida armchair with footrest** (~300 zł), a **MoodMe Scandi outdoor rug 120×180 cm** (~100 zł), and two grey cushions. This is the "slow Sunday morning coffee" build.

### Concept D — Urban Jungle Premium (~2 223 zł)
Concept B base but the plants take over. Five ivy plants, hanging macramé planters × 3, hanging KADAX pots × 2, herbs (basil, rosemary, thyme) on the IVAR shelves, and ferns. The **Lumiled solar string light with remote** (90 zł) adds warm evening ambiance. Dense, lush, and a bit wild.

## My Take

I keep coming back to **Concept C** for the vibe — the lighting makes evenings out there genuinely nice. But **Concept B** is probably the smarter long-term choice because the Keter bench solves the storage problem that a small balcony always has.

Still deciding. The deck tiles are going in regardless.
